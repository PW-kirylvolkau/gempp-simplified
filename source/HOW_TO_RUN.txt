BUILD:
    scripts\build.bat

RUN:
    gempp.exe [options] <input_file.txt>

OPTIONS:
- `--time`, `-t`: Show computation time in milliseconds
- `--fast`, `-f`: Use greedy heuristic (fast upper bound). Recommended for |V| > 15.
- `--ged`,  `-g`: Compute graph edit distance (for ILP only)

EXAMPLES:
    gempp.exe --time tests\01_triangle\input.txt
    gempp.exe --fast --time tests\01_triangle\input.txt
